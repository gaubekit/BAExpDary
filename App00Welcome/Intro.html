Thank you for participating in this research study: "Investigating the Relationship Between Time-on-Task-Induced Mental Fatigue and Choice Reaction Time".

Estimated total duration: about 2 hours.

How the session is structured

  1. Block 1 (≈ 15 minutes, Baseline): questionnaires and several assessment tasks to establish your baseline performance.
  2. Block 2 (≈ 90 minutes): the longest part, with a series of assessment tasks followed by a mental fatigue segment (MATB-II) and questionnaires.
  3. Block 3 (≈ 15 minutes, Post): same as Block 1 to compare with the baseline.

Before you begin

  • Follow the on-screen instructions for each task.
  • Proceed in the order presented and continue when you feel ready.
  • Minimize distractions to ensure accurate results.
